# Audio credits

## `waves-60s.mp3`

A 60-second segment of water-on-shore sounds, trimmed from `Waves.ogg` on Wikimedia Commons.

- Source: https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/File:Waves.ogg
- Author: Wikimedia user Dsw4
- Original recording: shotgun-mic recording on the shore of Lake Ontario in Webster, NY, autumn 2008
- License: Public domain (released by the copyright holder)
- Processing: ffmpeg, `-ss 30 -t 60` with a 2s fade-in and 3s fade-out, encoded mp3 96 kbps stereo

Duplicated from `apps/kalooban-quiz/audio/` so this app is self-contained. Replace both copies if you swap the audio file.
